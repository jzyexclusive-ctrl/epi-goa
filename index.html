Essential Travel Guide

Everything you need to know before you pack your bags.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Goa is between mid-November and mid-February. The weather is pleasantly cool and comfortable, perfect for relaxing on the beaches. If you prefer lush greenery and fewer crowds, the monsoon season offers a magical atmosphere.

Budget & Expenses

Goa caters to all budgets. Backpackers can manage on ₹2000/day. Mid-range travelers should budget ₹5000-8000/day. Luxury travelers can expect to spend ₹20,000+ per day. Cash is still widely used in smaller shacks, though UPI is commonly accepted.

Local Transport

The best way to explore Goa is by renting a scooter or motorcycle. You must carry a valid driving license and wear a helmet. Cabs (local private taxis or GoaMiles app) are available but can be expensive.

Safety Tips

Goa is generally very safe. Standard precautions apply: don't leave belongings unattended on the beach, avoid isolated areas late at night, and always swim in zones marked safe. Never drink and drive.

Do's and Don'ts

  • Do: Respect local culture when visiting temples and churches.
  • Do: Try the local Feni and Goan fish curry.
  • Don't: Use plastic bags on the beaches.
  • Don't: Take photographs of locals without permission.
